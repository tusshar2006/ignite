Book Reviews

The Song of Ice And Fire

by George R R Martin

A novel set in the IronAge with a twist of fantasy and chaos that tells the story of a throne and crown to rule all the kingdoms whose swords were forged in dragons' breath, where backstabbing , cheating , crookery, greed , anger ,strength , magic , influence are all at an all time high for one non living throne which proves the telling as said "Everything is fair in love and war." over and over again throughout.

THE LORD OF THE RINGS

by J R R TOLKEN

A novel that revolves into the dangers of totalitarianism, surveillance, and loss of individual freedoms in a tightly controlled society that is brought to peace and harmony with the help of powers, strengths and influence of a set of 3 rings.

ROBINSON CRUSOE

by DANIEL DAFOE

A story based in the 16th century where a boy from an affluent family thinks escaping his family would get his freedom and later realizes when he is captured as a slave and made to work on a ship that later wrecks and kills every crew member apart from him leaving him on a flourishing island far away from mainland and the story that goes ahead with how he discovers ways to survive on the island and discovers himself and his freedom and just as when he thinks he is all alone on the island he finds himself following a path of footsteps that are not his and learns something else on island the revolves with him being alone on the island, to finding a companion and also finding his way back to the mainland and leading a respected life of a merchant .
